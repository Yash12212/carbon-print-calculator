Understanding Your Carbon Footprint

Your carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases (including carbon dioxide and methane) that are generated by your actions. The main contributors to your personal carbon footprint include:

  • Transportation (daily commute, air travel)
  • Home energy use (electricity, heating)
  • Diet and food choices
  • Consumer habits

By tracking and reducing your carbon footprint, you can help combat climate change and create a more sustainable future.
