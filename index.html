How the Calculations are Made

Carbon Footprint: Your carbon footprint is calculated based on two main factors:

  • Commute Distance: Every km traveled by car emits an estimated 0.25 kg of CO₂.
  • Electricity Usage: Each kWh of electricity consumed emits about 0.7 kg of CO₂.
  • Gas Usage: Each m³ of natural gas used emits about 1.9 kg of CO₂.

Water Usage: We assume that for every kg of CO₂ emitted, your water usage increases by about 0.1 liters.

Waste Reduction: Similarly, for every kg of CO₂ emitted, your waste reduction is estimated to increase by about 0.05 kg.
